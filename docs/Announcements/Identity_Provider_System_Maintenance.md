We are strengthening access to REANNZ HPC services (Mahuika and OnDemand) by restricting login access to researchers who have appropriate compute allocations.

Researchers with Freezer-only allocations (tape storage) will no longer be able to log in to these systems. Instead, they should use the Globus Freezer mapped collection to transfer data to and from Freezer.

For more information please see our [Globus documentation](../Data_Transfer/Globus/Globus_Overview.md) or {% include "partials/support_request.html" %}.
